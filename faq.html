Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GoFlexer App?

The GoFlex app is a mobile application designed to provide a seamless, transparent, and secure solution for booking and managing tasks efficiently. Our app ensures that users can achieve their goals reliably and with ease.

Does GoFlexer work on iOS?

Yes, it does! GoFlexer is available on iOS and can be downloaded directly from the App Store. Make sure to check for updates to keep the app functioning optimally.

How can I contact the Support Team?

You can contact our support team through the in-app Feedback section or via email at admin@goflexer.com.au.

We are a dedicated team working tirelessly to assist you. Typically, you can expect a response within 24 to 36 hours. Feel free to reach out to us at any time—we’ll be glad to help!

Does GoFlexer support background mode?

Yes, it does run in the background. You can start the bot and close the app it will run in the background.

What happens if I need to forfeit a task?

Forfeiting tasks is simple with GoFlexer. Navigate to the "Bookins Page" tab in the app. Slid the block you want to forfeit and we will not charge you if you forfeit the block in 20 minutes of booking. If you forfeit tasks outside of GoFlexer and are still charged, contact our support team, and we’ll ensure that your fees are adjusted properly

How much does GoFlexer cost?

GoFlexer charges a 2.9% commission on the total price of a block. You’ll only be charged once the accumulated commissions reach $20.

Enjoy Your Experience!

We hope GoFlexer becomes a valuable tool and an enjoyable part of your workflow. If you have feedback or would like to rate our app, please visit our pages on the App Store or Play Store. Your support means the world to us! Thank you from the entire GoFlexer team!
